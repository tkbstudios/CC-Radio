What Exactly is CC: Radio?

CC: Radio allows you to play music tracks in ComputerCraft or CC:Tweaked without downloading them!
The script requests an audio file from our servers and plays it!
the advantage of this is that you don't need to have all the audio downloaded on your floppy disk or computer.

Is the music licensed?

No, we selected tracks where the audio is not licensed
